Understanding Child Malnutrition

Malnutrition in children occurs when they don't get adequate nutrients for growth and development. It can lead to stunted growth, weakened immunity, and developmental delays. Early detection is crucial for timely intervention.

Why Early Detection Matters

Early identification of malnutrition allows for immediate nutritional support and medical intervention. Our AI-powered system analyzes key health indicators to provide accurate assessments and recommendations.

Comprehensive Health Monitoring

Beyond malnutrition detection, our system helps track overall child development through BMI calculations, growth monitoring, and personalized health insights for better outcomes.
