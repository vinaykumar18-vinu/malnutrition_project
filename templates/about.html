About Our Mission

Our Technology

We leverage advanced machine learning algorithms trained on extensive pediatric health data to provide accurate malnutrition risk assessments. Our AI model considers multiple factors including age, weight, height, and mid-upper arm circumference (MUAC).

Expert Collaboration

Developed in partnership with pediatric nutritionists and healthcare professionals, our system follows WHO guidelines and international standards for child growth monitoring and malnutrition classification.

Impact & Reach

Our goal is to make malnutrition detection accessible worldwide, especially in underserved communities where traditional screening methods may be limited. Early intervention can save lives and improve long-term health outcomes.

Data Privacy & Ethics

We prioritize the privacy and security of all health data. Our system processes information locally and adheres to strict ethical guidelines, ensuring that sensitive medical information remains confidential and secure.
