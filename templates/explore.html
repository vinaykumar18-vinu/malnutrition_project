Key Health Indicators We Monitor

  • Age: Critical factor in growth velocity and nutritional requirements
  • Weight: Primary indicator of nutritional status and growth
  • Height: Essential for BMI calculation and growth assessment
  • MUAC: Mid-upper arm circumference for acute malnutrition detection
  • BMI: Body mass index for overall nutritional health evaluation

WHO Classification Standards

Our system follows World Health Organization guidelines for malnutrition classification:

  • Normal: BMI appropriate for age and height
  • Moderate Malnutrition: Requires nutritional support and monitoring
  • Severe Malnutrition: Immediate medical intervention needed
